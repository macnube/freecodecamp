Howard Zinn

Historian, Author, Activist, Global Citizen...Role Model for All

Timeline

  • August 24, 1922 - Born in Brooklyn, New York
  • 1940 - Graduated highschool and started working in apprenctive shipfitter in New York Navy Yard
  • Early 1940s - Zinn joins Air Force in fight againt Nazi's
  • April 1945 - Dropped napalm unknowingly on Royan France...an event that later shaped his anti-war stance
  • 1948-1951 - Attends New York University on GI Bill.
  • 1952 - Earns M.A. from Columbia University in history
  • 1956-1963 - Professor of history at Spelman College in Atlanta. A black university.
  • 1958 - Earns Ph.D in history and minor in political science
  • 1963 - Zinn was fired from Spelman for his participation in Civil Rights movements and encouraging his students
